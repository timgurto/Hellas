Welcome to Hellas—A free MMO based in ancient Greece

A free-to-play MMORPG set in Ancient Greece, inspired by Classic World of Warcraft and TerraFirmaCraft.

Join the Adventure

Download the client

Unzip and run the launcher. Once in-game, you can create an account for free.

About Hellas

Explore the legends of Ancient Greece, making contact with distant lands and battling enemies from history and myth.

Place permanent structures to make your mark on the world, from small pots to enormous monuments.

Advance from a brutish caveman to a bronze-age champion, through an extensive crafting system.

Engage with others in prosperous trade or savage warfare.
